Serving the Heart of UMSKAL

D GourMeals is a specialized Campus Food Delivery Network prototype, developed for the Labuan International Campus community.

Our Journey

Founded by a dedicated group of Business Computing (HC13) students, D GourMeals was born out of a mission to provide UMSKAL residents with high-quality dining options right at their doorstep.

Why UMSKAL Students Love Us:

  • Fast Delivery to residential colleges.
  • Student Prices for every budget.
  • AI Assistant 24/7 support.
  • Extended Hours until 9 PM.

Our Journey from idea to E-commerce

Follow the development journey of the D GourMeals platform from concept to launch.

Phase 1: Idea Generation

Week 1-2

We identified the problem of UMSKAL students having difficulty getting fresh food during peak hours. The original idea was to create a "digital bridge" between food vendors and residential colleges. We conducted an initial survey of 35 respondents to validate the need.

Google Forms Brainstorming Market Research

Phase 2: Wireframing & UI/UX Design

Week 3-4

Created complete wireframes for all pages using Figma. Focused on an intuitive user experience for students on the go. Green-turquoise theme colors were chosen to represent the freshness of the food, with orange accents for action.

Figma UI/UX Design Prototyping

Phase 3: Pembangunan Frontend

Week 5-7

Developed user interface using HTML5, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. Developed complex shopping cart system with localStorage. Created Menu pages with dynamic subcategories for a smooth browsing experience.

HTML5 CSS3 Grid/Flexbox JavaScript ES6 LocalStorage API

Phase 4: AI Chatbot Integration

Week 8

Meets the IE12103 course requirement for AI-based functions. GourBot AI is developed with a contextual response system for common questions. This chatbot is capable of understanding questions in Malay and English regarding menu, prices, and delivery.

Natural Language Processing Pattern Matching AI Logic

Phase 5: Testing & Launch

Week 9-10

Usability testing conducted with 15 UMSKAL students. Bugs in the shopping cart system fixed. Website optimized for mobile and desktop. Prototype ready for final demonstration with complete e-commerce features: catalog, cart, checkout, and AI support.

Usability Testing Bug Fixing Performance Optimization
