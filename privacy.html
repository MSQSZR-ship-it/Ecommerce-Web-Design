Privacy Policy

D GourMeals respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our campus food delivery platform users.

Academic Disclaimer: All data processed through this system is for the purpose of the Business Computing (HC13) UMSKAL course project by D Gour Meals Members.

Information Collection

We collect basic information such as your matrix number, phone number, and delivery location within the UMSKAL campus to ensure orders are delivered to the correct individual.

Use of Information

Your data is used exclusively to process food orders, calculate total payments, and provide delivery status updates.

Data Protection & Security

Your login status and cart details are securely stored in your browser's Local Storage. We take measures to prevent unauthorized access to your active session.

Payment Security

Please note that all transactions on the Payment page are dummy simulations. We do not request or store actual banking information, such as bank passwords or CVV numbers.

Cookies & Local Storage

We utilize the D_GourMeals_Cart_v5 storage key to temporarily save your order information until the transaction is completed or the cart is cleared.

Data Sharing

D GourMeals will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties outside of this project ecosystem.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our service. Users are encouraged to review it regularly.
