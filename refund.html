Refund Policy

At D GourMeals, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every order. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be requested.

Order Cancellation

Refunds are only available for orders that are cancelled before the preparation or delivery process begins. Once the kitchen starts preparing your meal, cancellation is no longer possible.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive the wrong item or your meal is damaged, please report the issue within 24 hours of delivery. We will evaluate the request for a dummy refund or a replacement order.

Refund Method

As this system uses a simulated payment gateway, any approved "refunds" will be credited back to your virtual account or handled as a manual transaction between the user and the vendor.

Delivery Fees

Please note that delivery fees are generally non-refundable unless the order issue was directly caused by a system error or delivery delay on our part.

Change of Mind

Refunds are not available for change-of-mind requests once the order has been delivered successfully to your location.
